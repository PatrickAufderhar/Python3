Lesson 6, Quiz 1

Question 1:
What are some advantages of compiled regular expression methods over re module functions?

Your Answer:
- Better performance over gigantic strings
- Code is more compact, more readable and more usable
Mentor Comments:
also more settings available, such as re.IGNORECASE

Question 2:
How would you compile the regular expression "\d{3}"?

Your Answer:
re.compile(r"\d{3}")

Question 2:
Which date system does the astronomy community use to track time?

Your Answer:
Julian date system

Question 3:
What is the difference between the strptime() and strftime() methods?

Your Answer:
strptime() converts strings to date objects, while strftime() converts date objects into desired format strings.

Question 4:
What is a timedelta?

Your Answer:
Timedelta is an object in the datetime library which represents the difference between two dates or times.
